A List of Movies that I like

In no particular order, here are some movies I've enjoyed watching at the top of my head

Smokin' Aces (2006)

This is the first movie that popped into my head to make this list. It's a movie I've watched before and have been recently craving to want to watch it again. This 2006 movie features a grand cast of individuals telling a story of how one man's ego got himself in hot water with the mafia who hired a mob of eclectic assassins to make sure he doesn't leave his suite alive.

Be Kind Rewind (2008)

This was the second movie to pop into my head for this list. It's a heartwwarming tale of friendship, creativity and belonging to a community. Jack Black and Mos Def are a great comedy duo and Danny Glover as the video store clerk.

The Florida Project (2017)

This third entry took some time to think of that was worthy enough for this list. John Wick and Terrifier were the honourable mentions that didn't make the cut. I really love this movie and find myself rewatching it every once in a while.

Blues Brothers 2000 (1998)

This is the last movie on this list and is probably the reason why I listed the movies like I did. I rented this movie when I was a young child living in Scarborough and thought the dealership in the movie looked familliar Turns out a lot of this movie was filmed in Toronto.
